Droplet motion tests

Four OLED-friendly animation directions for a cuter water pet. Each screen is drawn at 128 x 64 pixels, then scaled up with crisp pixels.

Floaty

Soft bobbing, big cheeks, gentle bubbles. This is the safest default idle.

Jelly

More squash and stretch, like a tiny water jelly. Cutest when reacting to a sip.

Sleepy

Slow drift and drowsy face. Good for long idle periods before reminding the user.

Bounce

Angry mode: it ricochets inside the screen, splashes on impact, then pulls itself together.
